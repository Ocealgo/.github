## About the brand

Ocealgo is a brand by **Paristhi Protech Private Limited**, exploring the use of marine and seaweed-derived ingredients in everyday personal care. We believe products that touch your body should come from places that respect the planet — and few resources are as abundant, renewable, and underused as the sea around us.
